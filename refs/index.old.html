About Me

My Work

And so I commit in the letters of my people; my story such as it is. Not long ago, I found myself ministering to the great ultracolds used by the Seton Tribe. they stored within them water that boiled when exposed to the air. Inside was the means to save a life. At the cost of one. 80% of all pediatric heart valves were under my watch.

Alas, that is no longer. Closed like the pages of book.  I then sought the wisdom of the of the fabricator. With my own eyes I watched as form was pulled up with will alone. Only once I had witnessed the greatness first hand of the industrial behemouth did I know my true calling. To commune with the divine. To speak the linga techna. The magic of code.
